North Bend Attorney

Donna Campbell has served Washington clients from King and Pierce counties, but mostly in the Snoqualmie Valley, since opening her law office in 2000. She values the importance of a good working relationship with her clients, and pursues their objectives with integrity. Donna is an accomplished Family Divorce Lawyer, Bankruptcy Attorney, and Traffic Law Attorney.

With a focus on traffic, divorce, family and bankruptcy law, she provides the services that people need when they are dealing with some of the most trying times in their personal lives. Her experience and personal attention to each client makes her the logical choice when you are in need of the services of an attorney.

Whether you are facing bankruptcy for the first time or are dealing with the devastating effects of a bitter custody battle, you need the right attorney on your side. Donna Campbell has a personal approach to her law services. She takes the time to get to know her clients, so you get representation that keeps your needs and goals at the forefront of the process.

The Campbell Law Office recognizes that each case is as unique. Donna takes the time to get to know your needs, creates a good working relationship and focuses on being as responsive as possible to those needs. Her clients can expect the personal attention they deserve for every step of the process.
